LocationCS CodeSystem

Location is describe as the point position of a mass was finding. Usually refers to the anatomical location of an organ. The goal of recording the location of the mass is that it can be used in image guided radiation oncology and surgical operation.

★ Topography location of breast

There is an element of One view only that interpreting as screening mammography in one view, there are generally two standard screening views used mainly for diagnostic evaluation of lesions in mammography, they are mediolateral oblique (MLO) view and craniocaudal (CC) view.


Pic 1. Two screening views of breast mammography		 ♢ MLO view is projion parallel to the pectoralis major muscle, and pectoralis should be seen to the level of or below the axis of the nipple and appear convex (never concave toward the nipple).
♢ CC view is Projection with slight rotation toward the sternum to detect posteromedial tumors that may be missed on the MLO view, lesion should be seen between axilla and medial of the breast.

Pic 2. Location point of breast mammograpgy
♢ UOQ: Upper outer quadrant
♢ UIQ: Upper inner quadrant
♢ LOQ: Lower outer quadrant
♢ LIQ: Lower inner quadrant
